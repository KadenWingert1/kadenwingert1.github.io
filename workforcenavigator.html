Workforce Navigator

Description

Workforce Navigator is a mobile app that can be run on iOS devices. It provides a solution for efficient employee management. Seamlessly navigate a comprehensive employee directory, allowing quick access and search capabilities for all team members. Additionally, the app consists of an attendance tracking system, enabling you to effortlessly mark daily attendance and maintain a detailed log for each employee. Workforce Navigator goes beyond simple attendance tracking by calculating weekly compensations and performing attendance analytics. Leveraging MongoDB as its backend, Workforce Navigator ensures secure and scalable storage of employee information, attendance records, and time off requests. Gain valuable insights, and enhance your overall workforce management with this powerful tool, designed to simplify your administrative processes and provide real-time visibility into your team's presence.

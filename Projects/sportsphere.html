Sport Sphere

Description

Sport Sphere is an android app where users can create public game posts which other users can join and interact with. It acts as convenient platform for people to schedule sporting events with others in the communinty. The app includes features such as adding comments in real time through websockets, a personal calender, and even a map showing the locatio of scehduled events. It also supports different functionality for various types of users including a regular user, game post creator, and admins.

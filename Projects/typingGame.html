Keystroke Rush

Description

Keystrok Rush is a fast-paced typing game developed in C that puts your typing skills to the test. Before the game begins, you are given the choice to select the minimum length of words that may appear to control the difficulty. As words cascade down the screen, you must swiftly and accurately type them before they reach the bottom. The game intensifies as the speed and complexity of the words increase, challenging players to stay focused and type with precision. When the game is lost, the final time is recorded and displayed to the screen.

dripdrop

Description

dripdrop is a social media-styled app enabling users to upload posts featuring clothing items. Each post includes detailed information like images, pricing, reviews, and similar product recommendations. Users can engage through likes, comments, and shares, cultivating a community around fashion trends. Targeted at fashion-savvy consumers, the app incorporates AI-driven suggestions to enhance product discovery. A custom-built AI model powers both the similar product recommendations and smart form autofill, streamlining the user experience by reducing manual input and surfacing relevant content more effectively.
